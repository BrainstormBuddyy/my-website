About Brainstorm Buddy

Brainstorm Buddy is your creative thinking companion — an AI-powered extension designed to turn messy sparks of inspiration into organized, actionable notes. Whether you're drafting an article, outlining a business pitch, or simply collecting thoughts, our tool helps you capture, refine, and explore ideas with speed and clarity — all from the comfort of your browser.

Why We Built It

Great ideas often strike when you're not expecting them — and traditional note apps can slow you down or leave you disorganized. We built Brainstorm Buddy to eliminate that friction. Our goal was to create a seamless, AI-enhanced space where creativity flows naturally, and ideas can evolve into action — without jumping between apps or losing your train of thought.

Who It's For

  • 🧑‍🎓 Students: Essay and study planning
  • ✍️ Writers: Break writer’s block and ideate
  • 💡 Entrepreneurs: Business and product notes

Our Mission

To empower anyone to generate, refine, and organize ideas — intelligently and instantly.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How does Brainstorm Buddy work?

It uses AI to generate ideas based on your prompts. You can also write, edit, and save notes in your browser.

Is there a free version?

Yes! The free version gives you access to essential features, allowing you to generate 10 AI ideas per month. You can upgrade for access to more advanced tools.

Can I export my notes?

Absolutely. You can export or import notes at any time from the extension.
