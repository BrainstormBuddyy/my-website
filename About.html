Why We Built It

Great ideas often strike when you're not expecting them — and traditional note apps can slow you down or leave you disorganised. We built Brainstorm Buddy to eliminate that friction. Our goal was to create a seamless, AI-enhanced space where creativity flows naturally, and ideas can evolve into action — without jumping between apps or losing your train of thought.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Brainstorm Buddy work? ▾
It uses AI to generate ideas based on your prompts. You can also write, edit, and save notes in your browser.
Is there a free version? ▾
Yes! The free version gives you access to essential features. You can upgrade to Basic, Pro, or Elite for more AI generations and advanced tools.
Can I export my notes? ▾
Absolutely. You can export notes as Markdown or plain text, and import from any text file — all from within the extension.
What AI analysis tools are included? ▾
After generating ideas you get access to 7 instant tools: Reverse Brainstorm, SWOT Analysis, Idea Ranker, Niche Drill-Down, What If Mode, Mind Map, and Trend Radar — all free, no extra credits.
Can I get a refund? ▾

Subscriptions: You may request a refund within 14 days of purchase. After that period, yearly and monthly subscriptions are non-refundable.

Policy notes: To prevent misuse, we may limit future purchases or decline refund requests if more than two have been approved for the same account or payment method.

Refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days.
