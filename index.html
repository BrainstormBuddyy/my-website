How it works

Up and running in 60 seconds

No setup. No learning curve. Just open and think.

1
Install the extension

Add Brainstorm Buddy to Chrome in one click from the Web Store. It appears instantly in your toolbar — no account required to start.

2
Type your challenge

Click the Brainstorm Buddy icon, type any prompt — a business idea, essay topic, campaign brief, anything — and hit Generate.

3
Analyse and refine

Use AI analysis tools to rank, stress-test, and drill into your ideas. Refine with one click or run a mind map to see the full picture.

4
Save, export, or share

Save ideas to your notepad, export as Markdown, record a voice note, or save any web page — everything in one place.
